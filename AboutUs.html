BRIEF HISTORY ABOUT INDEPENDENCE CHORALE

Mr David Bashir, a music icon and energetic Music Conductor is the founder and Music Director of INDEPENDENCE CHORALE ABUJA. He hails from the almighty BENIN KINGDOM that is found in the south south part of Nigeria.

Independence chorale started in the year 2021 when few passionate singers came together as a group, seeking to explore the world of choral music and through supports and partnership, Independence chorale Abuja was formed. We started off by covering events such as weddings, birthdays, and also performing at christmas carol concerts and special gatherings. The name “INDEPENDENCE” was chosen to project the chorale as one that seeks to have a NATIONAL RELEVANCE and also become a household name to everyone.
